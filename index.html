Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This privacy policy describes how this application ("the App") accesses, uses, and protects information through the Pinterest API.

Disclaimer

This App uses the Pinterest API. It is an independent, personal-use tool and is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or officially connected to Pinterest, Inc. in any way.

Overview

This App is a personal-use tool that automates the creation and scheduling of Pinterest pins on behalf of a single account owner. It is not a public-facing service, and it is not used to collect or process data on behalf of any other individuals or organizations.

Information We Access

Through the Pinterest API, the App accesses:

  • Pinterest account authentication tokens (OAuth) required to create and schedule pins
  • Board and board section information, used to determine where pins are posted
  • Pin content submitted by the account owner (images, titles, descriptions, links, and alt text)

How Information Is Used

Information accessed through the Pinterest API is used solely to:

  • Create and schedule pins on the account owner's own Pinterest boards
  • Organize pins into the correct boards and sections

No data obtained through the Pinterest API is sold, rented, resold, or redistributed to any third party. No Pinterest-derived data is used for advertising or marketing purposes outside of the account owner's own Pinterest account.

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored securely and used only to authorize API requests to Pinterest. Pin content (images, titles, links) is sourced from third-party product listings (eBay) and is not stored beyond what is necessary to complete the posting process.

Third-Party Services

This App interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Pinterest API — for creating and scheduling pins
  • eBay — as the source of publicly available product listing information (titles, images, descriptions) used to generate pin content

This App does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices of Pinterest or eBay. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since this App operates on behalf of a single account owner for personal use, any stored data (such as authentication tokens) can be deleted at any time by revoking the App's access through Pinterest account settings.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in how the App operates. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
